Program

The Stabilization Phase: A Foundation for Recovery

As the initial phase of our inpatient rehabilitation program, our stabilization facility is designed for clients who:

  • Test positive for substances
  • Are experiencing withdrawal symptoms
  • Have mental health issues that require close monitoring

This crucial stage provides a safe environment for thorough physical dan mental observation, ensuring clients are prepared to move on to the next phase of their recovery journey. Our stabilization rooms are fully equipped to support clients through this critical process.

Inpatient/Residential Rehabilitation

Satu Nusa’s inpatient rehabilitation program provides a safe, comfortable, and supportive environment for individuals on their journey to recovery from all forms of addiction and mental health issues. To meet diverse client needs, we offer three distinct types of residential facilities, setting us apart from other centers:

  • Private Rooms: For clients seeking maximum privacy and solitude during their recovery.
  • Shared Rooms: For clients who benefit from peer support and a communal living environment.
  • Private Villas: For clients who require an exclusive and comprehensive residential experience.

Outpatient Rehabilitation

Our outpatient program offers a safe and supportive environment for individuals dedicated to recovery from addiction and mental health issues. This flexible option allows you to maintain your daily responsibilities, stay connected to family, and continue with work or education while receiving expert care.

  • Regular Outpatient: A structured weekly schedule with therapy and support sessions.
  • Intensive Outpatient: A more frequent, in-depth program for those requiring a higher level of support.
  • Online Outpatient: Convenient, remote care and therapy sessions for individuals who need flexibility due to distance or scheduling conflicts.
