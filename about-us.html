About Us

BACKGROUND

Yayasan Sayap Tumbuh Nusantara, hereinafter referred to as Yayasan SATU NUSA is a non-profit organization established by a community consisting of professionals who care about the problem of drug abuse and illicit circulation in Indonesia. Starting its activities in Bali Province since September 12, 2022, the SATU NUSA Foundation packs recovery treatments for victims of drug abuse and illicit circulation...

Currently the SATU NUSA Foundation is located in South Denpasar District, precisely in Serangan Village, with various supporting facilities and infrastructure for rehabilitation treatment activities such as; Multipurpose Room, Prayer Room, Bedroom with a capacity of ten patients and patients, Staff Workroom, Seminar Room, and Cons Room...

It is realized that every activity must have a legality umbrella...

VISION

“As a recovery house and social rehabilitation center for members of society, we aim to assist in the recovery of individuals struggling with addiction to narcotics, alcohol, gambling, online gaming, sexual relationships, pornography, and love through holistic therapy.”

MISSION

Our mission is to help individuals struggling with addiction to narcotics, alcohol, gambling, online gaming, sexual relationships, pornography, and love achieve holistic well-being based on the eight pillars of wellness, which include:

  • Emotional Well-being: identification and management of emotions.
  • Spiritual Well-being: search for purpose and meaning in life.
  • Intellectual Well-being: brain health and growth through mentally stimulating activities.
  • Physical Well-being: health related to physical aspects through a healthy lifestyle.
  • Environmental Well-being: harmony with natural environment and cultural wisdom.
  • Financial Well-being: aspects that support financial stability.
  • Occupational Well-being: job satisfaction and personal growth.
  • Social Well-being: social values, norms, interactions, and relationships.
