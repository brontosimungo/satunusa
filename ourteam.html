Our Team

Delly Perwira Putra, S.Sos

Counselor

Delly is a distinguished professional in the field of addiction recovery. A native of Jakarta who grew up in Pekanbaru, he holds a Bachelor’s degree in Sociology from the University of Riau. Drawing from his personal journey of recovery, he has been clean from substance use since 2013, dedicating his life to helping others. His professional experience includes roles as a Program Manager and Senior Counselor at both government and non-profit rehabilitation centers. His expertise is recognized through numerous certifications, including as a certified Social Welfare Worker, and specialized training in Motivational Interviewing, rehabilitation management, and the neuro-psychology of addiction.
